Legality Of Emulation

According to US DMCA Law, An Emulator Isn't Illegal As It Doesn't Contain Any Copyrighted Code. However, Downloading ROMs Or Hosting ROMs Online Is Illegal.

Q/A

Q: How Can I Legally Get ROMs?

A: You Can Legally Rip ROMs Off Of A Cartridge Or Disc You Own.

Q: An Emulator Said I Need A BIOS?

A: Some Emulators Require A BIOS File As They Cannot Make The Emulator Function Without It. The Reason That They Do Not Provide It Out-Of-The-Box Because The Manufacturer Usually Would Copyright The Code.
